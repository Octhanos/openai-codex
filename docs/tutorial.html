Tutorial how to create your own API Key

Creation Date: February 4, 2023
Created By: Octavio Lopez
View most recent version on Tango.us
​
​

1. Go to https://openai.com/api/

2. Click on SIGN UP

3. Click on Email address

4. Paste your email into input

5. Click on Continue

6. Type password

7. Click on Continue

8. Type your name

9. Type your last name

10. Click on Continue‍

11. Enter your phone number

12. Click on Send code‍

13. Click on option

14. Click on Personal Account

15. Click on View API keys

16. Click on Create new secret key‍

17. Click on OK‍
